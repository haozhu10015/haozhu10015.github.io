About me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Neurorobotics Group led by Prof. Joschka Boedecker, co-advised by Prof. Moritz Diehl.

My current research involves the following topics.

  • Mathematical optimization:

    • Convex analysis and optimization

    • Convex optimization for scientific computation

    • Modeling languages for mathematical optimization problems

  • Machine learning:

    • Machine learning with regularizations and constraints

    • Inverse and constrained reinforcement learning
