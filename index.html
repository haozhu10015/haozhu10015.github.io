About me

I am a Ph.D. student at the Neurorobotics Group led by Prof. Joschka Boedecker, Department of Computer Science, University of Freiburg.

My current research involves the following topics.

  • mathematical optimization:

    • convex analysis and optimization

    • convex optimization for scientific computation

    • modeling languages for mathematical optimization problems

  • machine learning:

    • inverse reinforcement learning

    • constrained reinforcement learning

  • neuroscience and psychology:

    • mathematical modeling of animal and human decision making behavior

    • neural decoding and manipulation

    • brain machine interface
